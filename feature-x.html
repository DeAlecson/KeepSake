How it works

Scan, tap,
and remember.

01
Scan the QR
Open your keepsake box. Scan the QR code inside to begin registration — this links your unique box to your account.
02
Tap the NFC chip
Hold your phone to your keepsake. The embedded NFC chip syncs with your QR scan to verify ownership of your piece.
03
Build their profile
Name, breed, a photo, a description, and a little message from your pet — that's all it takes to create a living memorial.
04
Only you can edit
Your QR code is the key. Only the registered owner can update or change the pet's profile. Your memory, protected.
05
Anyone can visit
Next time someone taps your keepsake, they visit your pet's page — and can browse the wider community of beloved pets.
06
Grow with the community
More features coming — reactions, guestbooks, anniversaries. This is just the beginning of an eternal bond.
